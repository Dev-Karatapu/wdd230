Overview

  • Small city located in Madison County, Idaho, United States
  • Home to Brigham Young University-Idaho (BYU-Idaho)
  • Population estimated around 28,000
  • Known for its community feel and scenic surroundings

Life

Rexburg offers a peaceful and family-friendly environment with various outdoor activities. It's known for its tight-knit community and supportive atmosphere for students.

Economy

The economy in Rexburg is largely centered around education due to the presence of BYU-Idaho. Other sectors include retail, healthcare, and agriculture, contributing to the city's growth.
