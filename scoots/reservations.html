Scooter Rental Terms and Conditions
  • Reservation Confirmation: To rent a scooter, a $30 upfront payment is required as a security deposit. This deposit ensures that you're serious about renting and will be refunded when you return the scooter.
  • Driver's License: Make sure you have a valid driver's license with you when you come to pick up the scooter. This is like permission to ride the scooter legally.
  • Age Limit: Only individuals who are 14 years old or older are permitted to ride the scooters. Safety first!
  • Fuel Charges: The rental price doesn't cover the cost of fuel. You'll need to pay extra for the fuel you use during the rental period.
  • Mileage: Ride the scooter as much as you like without any additional charges. Enjoy the ride to the fullest!
  • Insurance: We provide liability insurance for accidents. If you want extra protection for any damage to the scooter, additional insurance is available for purchase.
  • Free Delivery: For rentals of two days or more, enjoy free delivery.
  • Payment Options: Pay for the scooter using Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo. Choose the method that suits you best.
  • Return Locations: After your ride, return the scooter to either of our locations. We've got options for your convenience!
